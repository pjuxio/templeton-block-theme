Find your why

At Templeton Academy, we seek students who are kind, curious, and resilient—young people eager to embark on a transformative educational journey where they'll discover their unique purpose and potential. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward while helping us get to know your student and your family.

How to Apply

  1. Create Profile / Submit Application Through Ravenna at ravenna-hub.com
  2. Experience Day Spend a day at Templeton to see our unique approach in action. Schedule your tour.
  3. Academic Records Current report card including at least Q1 grades
    Prior year report card
  4. Teacher Recommendation
  5. Student Video or Essay Sharing their interests and aspirations
  6. Family Interview

Important Dates & Deadlines

  • Priority Deadline
    January 15, 2026
  • Rolling Admissions
    After January 15, applications accepted on a rolling basis

    Students have two weeks to register
  • Late Registration
    Contact admissions for availability

Questions
