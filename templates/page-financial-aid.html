Tuition & Financial Aid

Making High-Quality Education Accessible

Templeton Academy is socio-economically diverse by design. Our goal is to be both high-quality AND highly accessible. We realize that our tuition is still not affordable to roughly 1/3 of the families that we serve without financial aid.

We're committed to providing a premium educational experience at a more accessible price point - in other words: if you want to attend Templeton Academy but don't know if it's possible for you, we want to help you find a way.

Tuition Information

Tuition is $35,000 per year for the 26/27 school year.

Families should also budget for textbooks, school supplies, laptop, special events, and optional overnight trips.

Financial Aid Process

Our financial aid applications are assessed through our partnership with Clarity, making the process simpler and more secure. Templeton Academy offers need-based financial aid to eligible families through our streamlined process with Clarity.

How to Apply for Financial Aid

Complete the Clarity Application

  • Our financial aid applications are assessed via our partnership with Clarity
  • The streamlined, mobile-friendly application can be completed in as little as 20 minutes
  • Your 2024 tax forms (1040 and W2) will be automatically transferred from the IRS
Clarity Family Application Guide

Important Information

First Come, First Served

  • Financial aid applications are assessed on a first come, first serve basis
  • Currently enrolled Templeton Academy students receive priority through January 31
  • Families must reapply each year for each student

Notification Process

  • Awards are issued in the form of tuition discounts
  • Students are notified of their award amount in their acceptance letter
  • Families have two weeks to accept the offer
  • Awards are forfeited if registration is not completed within one month of acceptance
